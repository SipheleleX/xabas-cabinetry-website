About Xaba's Cabinetry

A family business

Xaba's Cabinetry (Pty) Ltd is a registered family-owned cabinet making business based in Gauteng. With 15+ years of hands-on experience, we've built our reputation on quality craftsmanship, honest pricing, and exceptional customer service.

We believe every home deserves beautiful, functional spaces — built to last. That's why every project starts with a detailed site visit and ends only when you're completely satisfied.

TRANSPARENT PRICING

No Surprises, No Hidden Costs

How Our Pricing Works

Every project is unique. Pricing depends on size of your project (measured in linear meters), materials selected (melamine, wood grain, high gloss, etc.), countertop choice (laminate, granite, Caesarstone), hardware & finishes, project complexity, and installation requirements.

We provide accurate, transparent quotes tailored to YOUR specific project - no guesswork, no surprises.

Why We Don't List Prices Online

Cabinet pricing isn't one-size-fits-all. Every project is unique, and accurate pricing requires understanding your specific space dimensions, materials you prefer, countertop selection, project complexity, and installation requirements. We believe in transparency - which means giving you ACCURATE pricing for YOUR project, not generic estimates that may be completely wrong for your needs.

OUR PROCESS

From Contact to Completion

1

Initial Contact

Reach out via phone, WhatsApp, email, or our website contact form. Tell us about your project - even rough ideas are fine!

2

Site Visit & Consultation

We visit your property to take accurate measurements, assess site conditions, discuss your needs, show material samples, and provide professional recommendations. Site fee is fully deducted if you proceed.

3

Detailed Quote

Within 2-5 business days, receive an itemized breakdown of all costs, materials specifications, timeline estimate, payment schedule, and terms. Quote valid for 30 days - no pressure!

4

Design Approval

Approve materials and design, confirm final measurements, sign quotation acceptance, and we answer any last questions.

5

Secure Your Booking

50% deposit payment secures your materials and reserves installation dates. We place orders with suppliers and confirm timeline.

6

Professional Installation

Our skilled team arrives on scheduled date, completes work professionally, ensures proper fitting, and performs daily cleanup. Progress payment (40%) due on start.

7

Final Inspection & Sign-Off

Inspect completed work, raise any adjustments needed, and sign off on completion. Final payment (10%) due. We're here if you need us!

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything You Need to Know

General Questions

Do you offer free quotes?

We charge a Site Measurement & Consultation Fee (R200-R500 depending on distance) which covers travel, time, and detailed measurements. This fee is fully deducted from your final invoice if you proceed with the project.

Why can't you give me a price over the phone?

Cabinet pricing depends entirely on your project size (measured in linear meters) and the materials you choose. We provide accurate pricing, not guesses - which protects both you and us from surprises later.

How long does a typical project take?

Timeline depends on project size: Small vanity: 1-2 days | Standard wardrobe (3-4m): 2-3 days | Medium kitchen (5-7m): 3-5 days | Large kitchen (8m+): 5-7 days. Total timeline from deposit to completion is typically 2-4 weeks.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Vereeniging and serve all around Gauteng. Contact us with your location for site visit fee details.

Do you do freestanding furniture?

No, we specialize exclusively in built-in cabinetry that's custom-fitted and permanently installed.

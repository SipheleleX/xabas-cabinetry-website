How our pricing works

Accurate pricing for
your project.

Every project is unique. Pricing is determined by the size of your project (measured in linear metres), materials selected (melamine, wood grain, high gloss), countertop choice (laminate, granite, Caesarstone), hardware and finishes, project complexity, and installation requirements.

We provide accurate, transparent quotes tailored to your specific project — no guesswork, no surprises, no hidden costs.

Why we don't list prices online

Cabinet pricing isn't one-size-fits-all. A 3-metre wardrobe and an 8-metre kitchen are completely different projects. We believe in transparency — which means giving you accurate pricing for your project, not generic estimates that may be wildly off for your actual needs. A site visit is the only honest way to quote.

What's included in every quote

  • Materials sourced from reputable suppliers — with quality guarantees
  • Demolition (if required) — priced per linear metre
  • Installation labour — calculated by project size and complexity
  • Transport from warehouse to installation site
  • Sundries — silicone, screws, fixings and consumables
  • Site visits and professional measurements
  • Basic layout drawings for review before approval

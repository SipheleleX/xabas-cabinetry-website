Our story

Where it all
began.

Xaba's Cabinetry (Pty) Ltd is a registered family-owned cabinet making business based in Gauteng. With over 15 years of hands-on experience, we've built our reputation on quality craftsmanship, honest pricing, and exceptional customer service.

What started as a passion for woodwork and built environments grew into a fully registered business committed to transforming Gauteng homes. Every project — from a single bathroom vanity to a complete kitchen renovation — receives the same level of care and attention to detail.

We believe every home deserves beautiful, functional spaces. That's why we specialise exclusively in custom built-in cabinetry solutions — fitted permanently to your exact measurements. No freestanding furniture, no compromises.

From your first inquiry to final sign-off, we're committed to transparency, professionalism, and results that exceed expectations.
